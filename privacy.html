AutoPost - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic account information provided by TikTok when you log in.
  • Access tokens required to schedule and publish videos.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your TikTok account.
  • To schedule and post your videos at the times you choose.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store sensitive personal information (e.g., passwords, emails, payment data).
  • We do not permanently store your videos after they are posted.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • Data is only shared with TikTok through their official API.

5. Security

  • We use industry-standard methods to protect your account data.
  • If TikTok access tokens expire or are revoked, AutoPost cannot access your account.
